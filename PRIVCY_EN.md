# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

WTools ("we", "our", or "the app") is a macOS utility application that enhances Finder with right-click context menu features. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**WTools does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.**

All operations are performed locally on your device:
- File creation, path copying, and terminal opening
- Settings and preferences are stored only on your Mac

## Finder Extension

WTools includes a Finder extension to provide right-click menu options. The extension:
- Operates only on files and folders you select
- Does not send any data to external servers
- Requires your permission via **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Extensions**

## In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases, payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment information. Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for details.

## Third-Party Services

WTools does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Changes
